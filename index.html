Time Alive Viewer

This is a Web App that visually shows you how long you've lived and how long you have left to live, assuming you're going to live 80 years...
Enter your birth date to see what you have lived and what you still have to live
The green dots are the weeks you have lived
The purple dots with numbers are the years you have lived
The gray dots are the weeks that you would have left to live
The dark gray dots with numbers are the years you have left to live
This is your day alive!
